# Content Calendar

## When to use this skill

Scheduling upcoming posts, choosing the next topic, or the weekly review
reaches its content step.

## Procedure

1. Review the last two weeks: what shipped, what slipped, what performed.
2. Keep a two-week runway of scheduled posts; backfill gaps from the idea
   backlog before adding new ideas.
3. One flagship piece per week; smaller posts fill the remaining slots.
4. Drafts follow the [personal style guide](../../../references/style.md).

## Boundaries

- Never schedule a post without a working draft attached.
- Quality over cadence: drop a slot rather than ship filler.
