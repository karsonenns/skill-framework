# Inbox Zero

## When to use this skill

The inbox needs processing, or the weekly review reaches its email step.

## Procedure

1. Process newest to oldest; touch each thread once.
2. For every thread pick exactly one: **reply now** (under 2 minutes),
   **task** (extract the action, archive the thread), **calendar** (schedule
   it), **archive**, or **unsubscribe**.
3. Replies follow the [personal style guide](../../../references/style.md).
4. Anything requiring more than 15 minutes of work becomes a task with a due
   date, never an open tab.

## Boundaries

- Never delete threads — archive only.
- Never unsubscribe from anything finance- or legal-related.
