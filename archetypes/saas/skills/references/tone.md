# Company Tone Guide

Shared org knowledge — skills link here instead of copying this text.

- **Plain language.** Short sentences. No jargon the customer didn't use first.
- **Own the problem.** "We charged you twice" — not "a duplicate charge occurred."
- **Lead with the resolution**, then the explanation, then next steps.
- **Never over-promise.** Give a time you can beat, not one you might miss.
