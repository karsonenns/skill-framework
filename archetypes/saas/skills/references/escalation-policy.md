# Escalation Policy

Shared org knowledge — skills link here instead of copying this text.

| Situation | Escalate to | SLA |
|---|---|---|
| Refund over $500 | Billing lead | Same business day |
| Legal threat or regulator mention | Legal | Immediately |
| Security or data-exposure claim | Security on-call | Immediately |
| Account >$50k ARR at risk | Account executive + CS lead | Same business day |

An escalation is a hand-off, not a broadcast: name a single owner and confirm
they accepted before closing your part.
